Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

Information We Store

Otterly stores family information that you enter, including:

  • Family member names and nicknames
  • Task descriptions and schedules
  • Points and reward information
  • Badge achievements
  • Activity logs (premium feature)

How Data is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, data is also stored in your private iCloud account.

We do not have access to your iCloud data. Apple manages the security of your iCloud storage.

Third-Party Services

Otterly uses Apple's services:

  • iCloud (optional sync between your devices)
  • App Store (subscription management)
  • iOS Calendar (optional import, premium feature)

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties.

Your family data stays with you. Period.

Children's Privacy

Otterly is a parent-administered family app. Parents create and manage all family member profiles. Children do not create accounts or provide personal information directly to the app.
